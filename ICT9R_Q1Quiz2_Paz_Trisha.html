The Wind Rises


A lifelong love of flight inspired by Japanese aviation engineer Jiro Horikoshi (Hideaki Anno), whose storied career includes the creation of the A6M World War II fighter plane. It was also based on ''The Wind Has Risen'' by Tatsuo Hori. Written by Hayao Miyazaki, it is mostly a fictionalised account of the engineer, and a subtle romance between the main character Jiro Horikoshi and Naoko Satomi, his wife. At the end of the movie, Naoko had went to Nagoya to evade the risk of Hirokoshi seeing her die to tuberculosis. It is truly a reflection of love and death, the consequence of art, how your work can even be used in a conflict you didn't even support yet your work somehow did, and as the creator, freedom has been taken from you for your creativity.
